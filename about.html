About Jimbo

Jimbo, better known in his small coastal town as Jimbo the Juice Man, was a vibrant character with an infectious enthusiasm for fresh, wholesome juice. His journey into the world of juicing began in his early twenties, when he inherited a quaint, rundown beach shack from his late uncle. Initially, Jimbo had no idea what to do with the property, given its state of disrepair and its seemingly impractical location, tucked away on a less-traveled part of the beach.

One scorching summer day, as Jimbo was cleaning out the shack, he stumbled upon an old, dusty juicer in the back room, a relic left behind by his health-enthusiast uncle. On a whim, he decided to plug it in and throw in some oranges that were a bit too ripe to eat but perfect for juicing. His favorites quickly became classic concoctions like carrot-ginger for a spicy kick, and watermelon-mint on those particularly hot days.
